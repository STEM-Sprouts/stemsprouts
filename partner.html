Partner with STEM Sprouts

We're on a mission to make STEM education free and accessible to every child. Join us in building the next generation of innovators and problem solvers.

Sponsorship Benefits

🌍

Brand Recognition

Your logo will be prominently displayed on our website, in workshop materials, and across our social media platforms, showing your commitment to community education.

🎯

Tax Benefits

As we operate under a 501(c)(3) fiscal sponsor, your financial contributions are tax-deductible, providing a meaningful way to support youth while meeting your social responsibility goals.

🚀

Direct Community Impact

Sponsorships directly fund STEM kits and equipment for students who might not otherwise have access to high-quality robotics or coding tools.

Ways to Support

There are many ways your organization can help STEM Sprouts expand its reach and impact.

Check out some of our current sponsors on the homepage.

  • Financial Sponsorship

    Funds go directly towards workshop materials, kits, and platform development.

  • In-Kind Donations

    Donate STEM kits, robotics hardware, or host space for our physical workshops.

  • Mentorship & Speaking

    Share your professional journey and expertise with students during our sessions.
